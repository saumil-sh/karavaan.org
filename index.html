Shipwreck

Do you think you can survive a shipwreck? See if you float or sink in this event.

You will be given the role of famous scientist or a personification of something science-y stuck in a sinking ship. To make it out alive you will need a life jacket but the ship is all out of life jackets. Lucky for you, the captain declares the he'll give his life jacket to anyone for who deems themselves worthy. CAN YOU convinve the Captain in the most innovative way for 'Why you deserve the life jacket over everyone else in the ship'?

Hackathon

(solving Healthcare and Sanitation Problems)

About

We face various challenges in Healthcare and Sanitation in Pune. Through this event, we plan to select five to eight pressing problems from this sector and solve it through the course of the event. The aim of the hackathon is to come up with scientific, technological or management related solutions to solve these problems. This Hackathon is your chance to learn how to take any idea from concept to creation within a matter of days!

Objective

The participants are expected to come up with either of the following

  1. Hardware Solution (prototype of the proposed device)
  2. Software Solution (An mobile based application/ Software)
  3. Case study ( Based on a cited Data)
The feasibility of the prototype solution and the quality of the effort put forward by the teams, will be the criteria for judgement.

Theme

Healthcare and Sanitation amalgamation of medical issues and health related issues arising from sanitation problems

Problem Details

To be decided by mentors on the day of Pre-hackathon

Pre-hackathon

  1. Panel discussion by mentors followed by Q&A session
  2. The panelists should try to illustrate the pressing problems/difficulties in the field.
About 5-8 problems would be selected as the topics for Hackathon.

Role and type of mentors

We are planning to bring in mentors from diverse backgrounds

  1. Government person
  2. NGOs
  3. Working in that sector (business or startup)
Mentors should be ready to
  1. Discuss and finalise the problems and present it during Pre-Hackathon (15th October2017, 1 pm to 4 pm) - Mentors discuss the problems amongst themselves, select 5-8 problem for the participants to work on, pitch it to the participants.
  2. Be present during the main hackathon for at least 4 hours - To guide the participants, consider the feasibility of the solution, provide them the ground-level facts/information.
  3. Judge the competition

Who is it for?

The event is open to everyone.

  1. Aspiring & experienced entrepreneurs
  2. Developers, designers and other tech-related professionals
  3. Non-technical field like business, sales, finance, legal, etc
  4. Students from colleges, universities, and even high schools
  5. Passionate visionaries
and many more!

Team Formation

  1. Maximum number of participants per team: 5
  2. Teams could be formed prior to the Hackathon or during the initial stage of the hackathon.

Important dates

15th October 2017 - Pre-hackathon (Announcement of final problem statements)
28th and 29th October 2017 - Hackathon

Prize to the participants:

  1. Laser engraved prize for winners
  2. Cash prize

About

Karavaan is the annual science, cultural, and social fest of IISER Pune. Born in 2008 as a small intra-college festival, it has grown with each edition and has now matured into something far greater, with more than 3000 students from colleges all across Pune participating in K'16.

The 10th edition of Karavaan is scheduled to occur on 3rd, 4th and 5th of November, 2017. These three days will be sprawling with a plethora of competitions and activities spanning areas like music, dance, photography, science, quizzing, drama, literary, and general tomfoolery. Eminent personalities from all over India from niche fields will also be invited to join us in a number of much-awaited talk series and workshops on a variety of subjects on the days leading up to, as well as during Karavaan. The theme for this year, Seasons, will in no small measure, leave a mark on the minds of everyone who joins us for the fest that is Karavaan.
